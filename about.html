About Big Dawg Pursuit, LLC

Veteran-owned drone services in Coastal Virginia

Who we are

Big Dawg Pursuit, LLC is a veteran-owned drone services company based in Coastal Virginia, focused on precision aerial operations and results-driven deliverables. The company was built around disciplined planning, attention to detail, and using the right tools for the job — not one-size-fits-all solutions.

Our work supports landowners, outdoor professionals, and project leads who need reliable aerial insight, clean data, and footage that serves a real purpose.

What we do

  • Precision drone operations for wildlife recovery support (where appropriate and legal)
  • Advanced 3D modeling for landowners (property visuals, planning, documentation)
  • Aerial photo & video (clean footage for land, property, and projects)
  • Land and site reconnaissance (layout, access routes, terrain features)
  • Project-specific deliverables (inspections, mapping, documentation, custom needs)

If you’re not sure whether a drone solution fits your project, reach out — we’ll talk through options.

How we work

Every project starts with understanding why the aerial data is needed — not just capturing footage. Operations are planned with safety, airspace awareness, and clear deliverables in mind.

FAA Part 107 certification is currently in progress. Operations are conducted with professional standards, responsible flight practices, and compliance awareness.
